Objectives
उद्देश्य

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

  • Identify optimization problems in real-world scenarios
  • Mathematically formulate linear programming problems
  • Graph feasible regions for systems of inequalities
  • Apply the corner point method to find optimal solutions
  • Analyze and interpret solutions in practical contexts
  • Recognize special cases in linear programming
  • Solve problems from business and economics using LPP
Teaching Methodology
शिक्षण पद्धति

Periods 1-2: Introduction to Optimization & Problem Formulation

  • Starter: Furniture dealer case study from NCERT
  • Concept: Objective functions and constraints
  • Activity: Formulating LPPs from real-world scenarios

Periods 3-4: Graphical Method & Feasible Regions

  • Demonstration: Graphing inequalities with GeoGebra
  • Group Work: Identifying feasible regions
  • Practice: Solving simple LPPs graphically

Periods 5-6: Corner Point Method

  • Concept: Theorem for optimal solutions at vertices
  • Case Study: Applying corner point method step-by-step
  • Worksheet: Calculating values at corner points

Periods 7-8: Special Cases in LPP

  • Analysis: Unbounded and infeasible regions
  • Discussion: Multiple optimal solutions
  • Activity: Identifying special cases in problems

Periods 9-10: Applications & Review

  • Practical: Solving business optimization problems
  • Historical: Linear programming in WWII operations
  • Assessment: Chapter test and project evaluation
