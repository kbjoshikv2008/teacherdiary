Content and Concepts
विषयवस्तु एवं संकल्पनाएँ
  • Area under simple curves
  • Area bounded by curve and lines (x = a, x = b)
  • Area bounded by curve and y-axis (y = c, y = d)
  • Areas when curve lies below x-axis
  • Area of circle and ellipse using integration
  • Area between two curves
Learning Outcomes (NCERT)
अधिगम उपलब्धियाँ
  • Calculate area bounded by curve, x-axis and ordinates
  • Determine area bounded by curve, y-axis and lines
  • Compute areas of standard curves (circles, ellipses)
  • Handle cases where curve crosses x-axis
  • Find area between two curves
  • Apply integration to solve real-world area problems

Objectives
उद्देश्य

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

  • Set up definite integrals for areas under curves
  • Calculate areas bounded by curves and axes
  • Compute areas of circles and ellipses using integration
  • Handle areas when curve crosses the x-axis
  • Determine area between two curves
  • Apply integration to solve real-world area problems
  • Interpret results in physical contexts
Teaching Methodology
शिक्षण पद्धति

Periods 1-2: Area Under Simple Curves

  • Starter: Compare geometric vs. calculus area calculation
  • Demonstration: Area as sum of infinitesimal rectangles
  • Activity: Calculate area under y = x² from 0 to 2

Periods 3-4: Area with Respect to Y-axis

  • Visualization: Horizontal strips concept
  • Group Work: Area bounded by y² = 4x, y-axis, y=3
  • Application: Physics displacement problems

Periods 5-7: Area of Circle and Ellipse

  • Concept: Deriving area formulas using integration
  • Case Studies: Engineering applications
  • Activity: Calculate ellipse area with different parameters

Periods 8-9: Areas Below X-axis and Between Curves

  • Concept: Absolute area when curve crosses x-axis
  • Problem Solving: Area between y = cosx and x-axis (0 to 2π)
  • Visualization: GeoGebra comparison of curves

Periods 10-12: Applications and Review

  • Practical: Economics surplus calculation
  • Review: Concept mapping of integration applications
  • Assessment: Chapter test and project evaluation
