Content and Concepts
विषयवस्तु एवं संकल्पनाएँ
3.1 Introduction to Trigonometry
  • Historical background and applications
  • Extension from trigonometric ratios to functions
3.2 Angles and Their Measurement
  • Positive and negative angles
  • Degree measure (1° = 60′, 1′ = 60″)
  • Radian measure (definition and relation with arc length)
  • Conversion between degrees and radians
  • Relation: π radian = 180°, 1 radian ≈ 57°16′
3.3 Trigonometric Functions
  • Unit circle approach: sin x, cos x definitions
  • Reciprocal functions: cosec x, sec x, cot x
  • Fundamental identities: sin²x + cos²x = 1, etc.
  • Signs of trigonometric functions in different quadrants
  • Domain and range of trigonometric functions
  • Behavior in different quadrants
  • Trigonometric values for standard angles (0°, 30°, 45°, etc.)
3.4 Trigonometric Functions of Sum and Difference of Angles
  • cos(x±y), sin(x±y), tan(x±y), cot(x±y) formulas
  • Double and triple angle formulas
  • Sum-to-product and product-to-sum identities
Learning Outcomes (NCERT)
अधिगम उपलब्धियाँ
  • Convert angles between degrees and radians
  • Apply the relationship between arc length, radius, and angle
  • Define trigonometric functions using unit circle
  • Determine signs of trig functions in different quadrants
  • Apply fundamental trigonometric identities
  • Use sum and difference formulas for trigonometric functions
  • Apply double and triple angle formulas
  • Solve trigonometric equations using identities
  • Understand historical development of trigonometry

Teaching Methodology
शिक्षण पद्धति
  • Session 1-2: Angle measurement and conversion (demonstration + practice)
  • Session 3: Unit circle introduction (hands-on activity)
  • Session 4: Trigonometric identities discovery (group work)
  • Session 5: Graphs and properties (digital visualization)
  • Session 6: Sum and difference formulas (guided derivation)
  • Session 7: Multiple angle formulas (problem-solving)
  • Session 8: Review and historical context (presentation + discussion)
  • Daily warm-ups with angle conversion problems
  • Exit tickets for formative assessment
