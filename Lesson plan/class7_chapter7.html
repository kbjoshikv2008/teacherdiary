Content and Concepts
विषयवस्तु एवं संकल्पनाएँ
- Introduction to triangles: vertices, sides, angles
- Construction of equilateral triangles
- Constructing triangles with given side lengths
- Triangle inequality theorem
- Constructing triangles with sides and included angle
- Constructing triangles with angles and included side
- Angle sum property and exterior angles
- Altitudes of triangles and their construction
- Classification of triangles: equilateral, isosceles, scalene, acute, right, obtuse
Learning Outcomes (NCERT)
अधिगम उपलब्धियाँ
- Students will understand properties of triangles
- Students will construct various types of triangles using compass and ruler
- Students will apply triangle inequality theorem
- Students will verify angle sum property of triangles
- Students will construct altitudes of triangles
- Students will classify triangles based on sides and angles
- Students will solve real-world problems involving triangles
Pedagogical Strategies
शैक्षणिक रणनीतियाँ
- Hands-on construction activities with compass and ruler
- Visual demonstrations of triangle properties
- Group problem-solving sessions
- Think-pair-share for concept exploration
- Real-life applications of triangle concepts
- Paper folding to demonstrate angle properties
- Error analysis in triangle constructions

Objectives
उद्देश्य
1. Understand properties and elements of triangles
2. Construct equilateral and other triangles accurately
3. Apply triangle inequality theorem
4. Verify angle sum property of triangles
5. Construct altitudes of various triangles
6. Classify triangles based on sides and angles
Teaching Methodology
शिक्षण पद्धति
Period 1: Introduction to triangles and equilateral triangle construction
Period 2: Constructing triangles with given sides and triangle inequality
Period 3: Constructing triangles with sides+angle and angles+side
Period 4: Angle sum property and exterior angles
Period 5: Altitudes of triangles and their construction
Period 6: Classification of triangles and problem-solving
