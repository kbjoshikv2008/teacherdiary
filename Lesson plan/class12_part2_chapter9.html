Content and Concepts

Objectives
उद्देश्य

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

  • Define differential equations and identify order/degree
  • Distinguish between general and particular solutions
  • Form differential equations from given families of curves
  • Solve first-order DEs using variable separable method
  • Solve homogeneous differential equations
  • Solve linear differential equations using integrating factor
  • Apply differential equations to model real-world problems
Teaching Methodology
शिक्षण पद्धति

Periods 1-2: Introduction and Basic Concepts

  • Starter: Real-life examples requiring DEs (population growth, cooling)
  • Demonstration: Order and degree of differential equations
  • Activity: Identifying order/degree in various DE examples

Periods 3-4: General and Particular Solutions

  • Concept: Verifying solutions of differential equations
  • Group Work: Finding particular solutions from general solutions
  • Application: Physics motion problems

Periods 5-6: Variable Separable Method

  • Technique: Separation of variables
  • Case Studies: Population growth, radioactive decay
  • Activity: Solving separable DEs with initial conditions

Periods 7-8: Homogeneous Differential Equations

  • Concept: Homogeneous functions and substitution method
  • Problem Solving: Solving homogeneous DEs step-by-step
  • Visualization: GeoGebra for solution curves

Periods 9-10: Linear Differential Equations

  • Technique: Integrating factor method
  • Demonstration: Solving linear DEs in standard form
  • Application: Electrical circuits, mixing problems

Periods 11-12: Applications and Review

  • Practical: Modeling real-world scenarios with DEs
  • Review: Concept mapping of solution methods
  • Assessment: Chapter test and problem-solving evaluation
