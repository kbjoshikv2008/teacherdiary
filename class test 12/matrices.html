Class Test: Matrices

Class 12 Mathematics | 20 Marks | 40 Minutes

Instructions:

  • Answer all questions
  • Show all necessary working
  • Marks are indicated against each question

1. If \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 2 & 1 \\ 2 & 0 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \), then show that \( A^3 - 6A^2 + 7A + 2I = 0 \).

[5 marks]

2. Given a square matrix \( A \) of order 3 such that \( A^2 = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & -4 & 4 \\ 0 & -1 & 0 \\ -2 & 2 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \), show that \( A^3 = A^{-1} \).

[5 marks]

3. If \( A = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & a & 2 \\ 1 & 2 & x \\ 3 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \) and \( A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & 1 \\ -8 & 7 & -5 \\ b & y & 3 \end{bmatrix} \), find the value of \( (a + x) - (b + y) \).

[5 marks]

4. If \( f(\alpha) = \begin{bmatrix} \cos \alpha & -\sin \alpha & 0 \\ \sin \alpha & \cos \alpha & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \), then prove that \( f(\alpha) \cdot f(-\beta) = f(\alpha - \beta) \).

[5 marks]
