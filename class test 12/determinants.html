1.(a) If \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & -2 \\ -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & -2 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \) and \( B^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & -1 & 1 \\ -15 & 6 & -5 \\ 5 & -2 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \), find \( (AB)^{-1} \).

[5 marks]

2. If \( A = \begin{bmatrix} -3 & -2 & -4 \\ 2 & 1 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \) and \( B = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 0 \\ -2 & -1 & -2 \\ 0 & -1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \), then find AB and use it to solve the following system of equations: \[ \begin{cases} x - 2y = 3 \\ 2x - y - z = 2 \\ -2y + z = 3 \end{cases} \]

[5 marks]

3. If matrix \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 2 & 1 \\ 4 & 1 & 3 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \), find \( A^{-1} \) and hence solve the following system of linear equations: \[ \begin{cases} 3x + 2y + z = 2000 \\ 4x + y + 3z = 2500 \\ x + y + z = 900 \end{cases} \]

[5 marks]

4. Using determinants, find the area of \( \Delta PQR \) with vertices P(3, 1), Q(9, 3) and R(5, 7). Also, find the equation of line PQ using determinants.

[5 marks]
