RoboCompass is a dynamic online tool for geometric constructions using a compass and straightedge approach. It allows users to create, animate, and share constructions with ease. Ideal for educational purposes, it visually demonstrates geometric principles in a clear and interactive way. It is widely used by students and teachers to explore Euclidean constructions, such as drawing perpendicular bisectors, angle bisectors, tangents, and more.
