Class 7 Mathematics

New Syllabus as per NEP 2020 & NCF-SE 2023
गणित प्रकाश (Ganita Prakash)

Chapter-wise Content

  • Chapter 1: Large Numbers Around Us
    Introduces the world of lakhs, crores, millions and billions through engaging contexts.
  • Chapter 2: Arithmetic Expressions
    Covers expressions with multiple operations and how to write/read them unambiguously.
  • Chapter 3: A Peek Beyond the Point
    Introduces decimal numbers and operations with them.
  • Chapter 4: Expressions using Letter-Numbers
    First steps into algebra with letter-numbers and algebraic expressions.
  • Chapter 5: Parallel and Intersecting Lines
    Fundamental geometry concepts with paper-folding activities.
  • Chapter 6: Number Play
    Computational thinking through puzzles, sequences, and cryptarithms.
  • Chapter 7: A Tale of Three Intersecting Lines
    Properties of triangles through construction activities.
  • Chapter 8: Working with Fractions
    Multiplication/division of fractions including Brahmagupta's methods.

Key Features:

  • Aligns with NEP 2020 and NCF-SE 2023 guidelines
  • Integrates Indian knowledge systems (e.g., Brahmagupta's methods)
  • Includes puzzles, real-life applications, and cross-subject connections
  • Develops computational thinking through interactive exercises
  • Available in both Hindi and English mediums

Class 11 Mathematics

Comprehensive foundation for higher mathematics

Chapter-wise Content

  • Chapter 1: Sets
    Introduction to sets and their properties.
  • Chapter 2: Relations and Functions
    Understanding relations and different types of functions.
  • Chapter 3: Trigonometric Functions
    Exploring trigonometric functions and their properties.
  • Chapter 4: Principle of Mathematical Induction
    Fundamental principle for proving mathematical statements.
  • Chapter 5: Complex Numbers and Quadratic Equations
    Working with complex numbers and solving quadratic equations.
  • Chapter 6: Linear Inequalities
    Solving and graphing linear inequalities.
  • Chapter 7: Permutations and Combinations
    Counting principles and their applications.
  • Chapter 8: Binomial Theorem
    Expanding binomial expressions using the binomial theorem.
  • Chapter 9: Sequences and Series
    Understanding arithmetic and geometric sequences and series.
  • Chapter 10: Straight Lines
    Various forms of equations of lines and their properties.
  • Chapter 11: Conic Sections
    Study of circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas.
  • Chapter 12: Introduction to Three Dimensional Geometry
    Coordinates in three dimensions and direction cosines.
  • Chapter 13: Limits and Derivatives
    Fundamental concepts of calculus.
  • Chapter 14: Mathematical Reasoning
    Developing logical reasoning skills in mathematics.
  • Chapter 15: Statistics
    Measures of dispersion and analysis of frequency distributions.
  • Chapter 16: Probability
    Basic concepts of probability and random experiments.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive coverage of fundamental mathematical concepts
  • Prepares students for higher studies in mathematics and sciences
  • Includes numerous solved examples and practice problems
  • Clear explanations with diagrams and illustrations

Class 12 Mathematics

Advanced concepts for senior secondary level

Chapter-wise Content

  • Chapter 1: Relations and Functions
    Types of relations and functions, composition of functions.
  • Chapter 2: Inverse Trigonometric Functions
    Properties of inverse trigonometric functions.
  • Chapter 3: Matrices
    Operations on matrices and their properties.
  • Chapter 4: Determinants
    Properties of determinants and their applications.
  • Chapter 5: Continuity and Differentiability
    Continuity, differentiability, and their applications.
  • Chapter 6: Application of Derivatives
    Rate of change, increasing/decreasing functions, maxima/minima.
  • Chapter 7: Integrals
    Methods of integration and their applications.
  • Chapter 8: Application of Integrals
    Area under curves and between curves.
  • Chapter 9: Differential Equations
    Formation and solution of differential equations.
  • Chapter 10: Vector Algebra
    Vectors and their operations in two and three dimensions.
  • Chapter 11: Three Dimensional Geometry
    Direction cosines, lines and planes in space.
  • Chapter 12: Linear Programming
    Formulation and solution of linear programming problems.
  • Chapter 13: Probability
    Conditional probability, Bayes' theorem, and random variables.

Key Features:

  • Advanced mathematical concepts for Class 12 students
  • Thorough preparation for competitive examinations
  • Clear explanations with step-by-step solutions
  • Includes exercises with varying difficulty levels
