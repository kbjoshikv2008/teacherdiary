Explore 3D Shapes

Cube

A cube has 6 equal square faces, 12 edges, and 8 vertices. All angles are right angles.

Cuboid

A cuboid has 6 rectangular faces, 12 edges, and 8 vertices. It resembles a box.

Sphere

A sphere is perfectly round and has no edges or vertices. Every point on its surface is equidistant from its center.

Cylinder

A cylinder has 2 circular bases and 1 curved surface. It has 2 edges and no vertices.

Cone

A cone has 1 circular base, 1 curved surface, and 1 vertex. It tapers smoothly from base to point.
